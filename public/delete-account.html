Delete your account

You can delete your CappingAlpha account and data at any time.

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete it yourself

  1. Log in to CappingAlpha (in the app or at cappingalpha.com).
  2. Open My Account.
  3. Scroll to Delete account, confirm, and your account is removed right away.

Prefer to email us

If you cannot log in, email support@cappingalpha.com from the address on your account and we will complete the deletion for you.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account we permanently remove your profile, tracked bets and notes, votes, comments, follows, notification subscriptions, referral records, and profile photo. Any active subscription is cancelled.

We keep only what the law requires us to retain (for example, records of past payments held by our payment processor for tax and accounting purposes), stored in a form that is no longer linked to a usable account.
